Mushroom Lasagna

Last updated 3 days ago

Delicicous lasagne with heaps of cheese

Tip: serve with rocket salade with tomatoes

Source: book - Plenty by Yotam Ottolenghi

Instructions

Mushroom mix

  1. Rest the porcini in a bowl with the luke warm water for 5 minutes

  2. Drain the porcini out of the water - saving the water for later

  3. Melt 60 grams of the butter in a large pan

  4. When the butter melts and foams, add the porcini and mushrooms

  5. Cook for 4 minutes until the mushrooms have lost some water and are limp

  6. Take the pan off the heat, and add the tarragon, parsley, salt and pepper

  7. Put everything into a pot, and set aside for later

Bechamel sauce

  1. Melt 60 grams of butter with the shallot for 1 minute on medium heat

  2. Stir in the flour, and mix until a roux is formed, but doesn't change colour

  3. Gradually mix in the milk and the porcini water into the roux

  4. Add 1.5 tsp salt and keep stirring until the saus is cooked

  5. When cooked, simmer for another 10 minutes on low heat until it smooth and thick

  6. Take the pan off the heat

  7. Save 3 tbsp from the sauce for the Ricotta mix

  8. Mix in the Swiss GuyÃ¨re until the sauce is smooth

Ricotta mix

  1. Whisk the egg and ricotta in a bowl

  2. Mix 3 tbsp of the prepared Bechamel sauce to the riccota and add the crumbed feta

The lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 celsius

  2. Add 1/5th of the Bechamel sauce in a lasagna dish (25cm x 25cm)

  3. Add a layer of 3 lasagna sheets

  4. Spread 1/4 of the ricotta mix on the sheets

  5. Add 1/4 of the mushrom mix

  6. Sprinkle on 1/4 of the mozzarella

  7. Repeat from step 1 for another 3 layers

  8. Add one last layer of lasagna sheets, with Beschamel sauce on top

  9. Sprinkle on the Parmezaan

  10. Cover the lasagna with aluminium foil so that it doesn't touch the top

  11. Cook for 40 minutes until the sauce is bubbling around the edges

  12. Take off the foil and cook for another 10 minutes

  13. Take the dish out of the oven, and let it stand for 10 minutes before serving
