Instructions

  1. Heat oil over medium heat in heavy skillet with high sides and a lid.

  2. Saute onion and pepper for about 5 minutes in a pan over medium heat until somewhat soft.

  3. Add garlic, paprika, cumin and cayenne/red pepper to taste. Saute for 2 more minutes

  4. Add tomatoes with their juices. Simmer over for 10-15 minutes until slightly reduced and thickened, stirring occasionally.

  5. Add spinach and feta. Reduce to weak simmer. For each egg, make a well in the sauce, and crack the egg into it. Put the lid on and continue to simmer for 10-15 minutes, until whites are cooked

  6. Remove from heat and serve immediately (so eggs don't overcook).

Ingredients

  • 1 onion - diced

  • 1 red bell pepper - chopped

  • 6 cloves of garlic - minced

  • 3 tbsp olive oil

  • 2 14oz cans diced tomatoes - undrained

  • 4 oz feta - crumbled

  • 1/2 bag spinach - roughly chopped

  • 4 eggs

  • 1.5 tsp smoked paprika

  • 1.5 tsp cumin

  • Cayenne and/or red pepper flakes.

  • Salt and pepper

  • Crusty bread or pita to serve
