Shakshuka

Heat oil over medium heat in heavy skillet with high sides and a lid. Saute onion and pepper about 5 minutes, until somewhat soft. Add garlic, paprika, cumin and cayenne/red pepper to taste. Saute 2 more minutes.

Add tomatoes with their juices, simmer over medium heat for 10-15 minutes until slightly reduced and thickened, stirring occasionally. Add spinach and feta.

Reduce to medium-low. Working one at a time, use a spoon to make a well in the sauce and crack an egg into it. Continue for all four eggs. Put the lid on and simmer for 10-15 minutes, until whites are set. Remove from heat and serve immediately (so eggs don't overcook).

Serve with crusty bread or pita.

1 onion, sliced. 1 red bell pepper, sliced. 6 cloves of garlic, minced. 3 tbsp olive oil. 2 14oz cans diced tomatoes, undrained. 4 oz feta, crumbled. 1/2 bag spinach, roughly chopped. 4 eggs. 1.5 tsp smoked paprika. 1.5 tsp cumin. Cayenne or red pepper flakes. Salt and pepper.
